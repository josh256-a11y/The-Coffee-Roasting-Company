Our Story

Crafting the perfect cup since 2003

Our Passion, Your Coffee

Founded in 2003 in Stellenbosch, South Africa, The Coffee Roasting Company began as a small passion project dedicated to hand-roasting ethically sourced beans in small batches. Over the past two decades, we’ve grown into a community-loved café and roastery known for our meticulous roasting process and dedication to quality.

From sourcing beans directly from farmers, to ensuring each roast highlights the bean’s unique origin, our mission has always been simple: to share exceptional coffee that connects people. Whether you enjoy a bold espresso or a smooth pour-over, we believe every cup tells a story.

What We Stand For

  • Quality: Carefully roasted beans for exceptional taste.
  • Sustainability: Partnering with ethical, eco-friendly suppliers.
  • Community: Creating spaces that bring people together.
  • Innovation: Exploring new brewing methods and flavors.
